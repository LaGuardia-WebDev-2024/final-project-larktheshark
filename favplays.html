FAVORITE MOVIES

Moonrise Kingdom

This has been one of my favorite movies since I was 10! It's about a girl who runs away from home and a boy who runs away from camp to be together, and escape their current lives due to dissatisfaction.

One Flew Over The Cuckoo's Nest

I watched this movie in seventh grade, it's about a psychiatric hospital and the patients who inhabit it. It shows the abuse of power that takes place in this hospital, and how a new patient affects everyone else there.

I actually watched this movie for the first time the most recently, as it was this year. I thought that there were a lot of heavy questions about morality throughout the whole movie that i found really intruiging, it's about a young delinquent who is sent to an institution to be changed, except the method they used was inhumane.
